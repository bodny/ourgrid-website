Fast, secure, easy to use and open-source!

Is an open source grid middleware based on a peer-to-peer architecture. This middleware enables the creation of peer-to-peer computational grids, and since its first release, in December 2004, it has been used by hundreds of users to speed up the execution of Bag-of-Tasks applications.
