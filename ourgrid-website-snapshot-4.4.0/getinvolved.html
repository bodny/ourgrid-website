Get involved with OurGrid

Do you want to get involved with OurGrid? There are several ways you can help us!

Report bugs and enhancements

  • By using our issue tracking system.
  • OurGrid Middleware Redmine: http://redmine.lsd.ufcg.edu.br/projects/ourgrid

Fix any bug or implement some enhancements

  • You can check out our code and start playing with it
  • OurGrid SVN (deprecated): http://svn.lsd.ufcg.edu.br/repos/ourgrid
  • OurGrid on GitHub: https://github.com/OurGrid/OurGrid

Subscribe our mailing lists.

  • OurGrid Users List: brings together people using OurGrid, so that they can discuss problems and features and share their experiences.
  • OurGrid Developers List: brings together people developing for OurGrid, so that they can discuss problems and features and share their experiences.

OurGrid Related Projects

Are you working on a cool project that uses the OurGrid? Are you developing a new feature that can enhance the way that the things work? Contact us and submit your work to the Related Projects section.
